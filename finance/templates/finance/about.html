ABOUT ME

Inspiration strikes in the most unexpected moments, and for me, it was a culmination of personal struggles and a burning desire to make a difference. As someone who has navigated the turbulent waters of personal finance, I understand the overwhelming challenges that come with managing money. This inspired me to embark on a journey of creating PerFM, a groundbreaking personal finance manager that puts the power back into your hands.

At its core, PerFM is more than just a project. It is a testament to the transformative impact personal experiences can have on innovation. The idea blossomed during my time at Holberton School, where I was given the freedom to explore my passion for technology and its potential to address real-life problems. This Portfolio Project became a conduit for turning my vision into reality.

Every aspect of PerFM has been meticulously crafted with you in mind. We recognize that financial management goes beyond numbers; it's a deeply personal journey. That's why we're committed to providing an intuitive platform that empowers you to take charge of your financial well-being.

Join us on this transformative journey and experience the power of PerFM. Together, we can revolutionize personal finance management and provide a solution that empowers individuals to achieve financial freedom.
